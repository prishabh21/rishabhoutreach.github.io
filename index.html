ALUMNI - BUDDY PROGRAM

Outreach Cell will help to make your college journey serene and more productive. We are launching the 4th edition of our flagship event for sophomore students, the Alumni Buddy Program, where you'll have a fantastic opportunity to interact with an alum personally. We understand that deciding whether to go for research, continuing with core, exploring finance, or trying for software roles is very confusing. And here, the alumni come to your rescue, with years of experience in their respective fields, clearing your doubts and guiding you in your career. An Alumni Buddy is like a godmother/godfather assigned to you who you can connect with for advice on several topics and help you advance towards your dream career.

Mock-en-Joy: Mock Interview Series

We all know that internships are an important part of one’s career. Not only are they a stepping stone into different domains but they are also a way to explore different opportunities and choose a future career path. But the process of getting an internship offer is tedious and time-taking. The arduous process of taking and clearing multiple tests and group discussions is followed by interviews. These interviews do not only require you to put up your best self before the interviewer but to also maintain composure and answer confidently. One needs to constantly be on the same page with the interviewer and answer accordingly, to perform well. The best way to prepare is to mock in a similar environment as the D-day, with constant feedback and criticism from the experts in the field, to help you improve yourself and give your best shot. Keeping all this in mind, Outreach Cell brings you the opportunity of facing an IITK Alumnus for a Mock Interview through its flagship event, "Mock-en-Joy,” where we have collaborated with our IITK Alumni who have ample experience in the profile you wish to work in, and they will be interviewing you from the same industry/field that you will be opting for in the interviews on D-Day
